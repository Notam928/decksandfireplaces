STEVE SLADE

DECKS AND FIRE PLACES

 

Over 40 years of experience

With over 40 years of experience in outdoor construction, Steve Slade has built a solid reputation for quality craftsmanship, reliability, and personalized service. Specializing in custom decks, fences, and outdoor fireplaces, Steve transforms ordinary yards into functional, beautiful outdoor living spaces tailored to each client’s needs.

Whether it's a modern composite deck, a sturdy privacy fence, or a natural stone fireplace, every project is approached with the same level of care and attention to detail. Based in Ottawa and serving Kanata, Rockland, Orleans, and the surrounding region, Steve personally oversees each step — from the first consultation to the final nail — ensuring that communication remains clear, timelines are respected, and finishes are flawless.

Clients appreciate Steve’s hands-on approach, deep expertise, and commitment to delivering results that last. At Steve Slade Decks & Fireplaces, your vision is our blueprint.
